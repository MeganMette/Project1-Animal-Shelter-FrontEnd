About Us

Welcome to the Yorkshire Animal Shelter.
Here at the shelter work tirelessly to campaign against animal cruelty. We rely on the support of all our staff, volunteers and most importantly you to help us push for changes in the law. This will help to improve the legal standards around the welfare of animals on farms, in labs, in the wild, in paddocks or our homes.
Through vigorous investigations and prosecutions we stand up to all those who deliberately harm animals in order to send out a clear message that we will NOT tolerate animal abuse. We have a team of highly trained officers who are always ready to tackle cases of neglect and cruelty on every level.

We are working to stamp out animal cruelty all together.

All animals will be able to rely on us to:
  • Rescue them when they need us most
  • To rehabilitate them wherever possible
  • Provide them with the very best veterinary care
  • To find them new homes, either through rehoming or release back into the wild
